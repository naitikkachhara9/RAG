NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (NDA)  
Date: March 15, 2025  
Disclosing Party: DataNova Analytics (“Discloser”)  
Receiving Party: PartnerX Labs (“Recipient”)

---------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE
---------------------------------------
The Parties intend to explore a potential collaboration involving joint research, product-integration opportunities, and exchange of certain technical, analytical, and business data. The discussions may involve proprietary algorithms, financial projections, software architectures, customer-segmentation models, and confidential product-roadmap items.

---------------------------------------
2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
---------------------------------------
Confidential Information refers to any information designated as confidential or that, under the circumstances, should reasonably be considered confidential. This includes, but is not limited to:  
- business plans,  
- model architectures and pipelines,  
- neural-network parameters,  
- prompt engineering strategies,  
- any written, oral, electronic, or visual information shared by the Discloser.

Confidential Information does not include information that:  
(a) is publicly available through no fault of the Recipient,  
(b) was independently developed without access to the Discloser’s information,  
(c) is already known to the Recipient before disclosure, as documented by records,  
(d) is required to be disclosed by law or court order.

---------------------------------------
3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECIPIENT
---------------------------------------
Recipient agrees to:  
(a) maintain confidentiality using at least reasonable care;  
(b) restrict disclosure only to employees or contractors with a legitimate need-to-know;  
(c) use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;  
(d) notify Discloser promptly upon discovering unauthorized access;  
(e) return or destroy all Confidential Information upon request.

Recipient shall implement controls such as access restrictions, encrypted storage, audit logs, and internal confidentiality policies.

---------------------------------------
4. TERM
---------------------------------------
This Agreement remains in effect for five (5) years from the date of last disclosure, unless extended by mutual agreement.

Confidentiality obligations survive termination.

7. RETURN OF MATERIALS
---------------------------------------
Upon request, Recipient shall return or securely destroy all Confidential Information, including digital copies, backups, derivative notes, and machine-learning outputs if they reveal confidential details.
