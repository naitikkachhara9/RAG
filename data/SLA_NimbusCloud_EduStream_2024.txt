SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA) — CLOUD HOSTING  
Effective Date: July 1, 2024  
Provider: NimbusCloud Hosting  
Customer: EduStream Pvt. Ltd.

---------------------------------------
1. SERVICE AVAILABILITY
---------------------------------------
Provider guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime for the cloud hosting platform, excluding scheduled maintenance. Scheduled maintenance shall be announced at least forty-eight (48) hours in advance.

Outages shall be measured based on periods where the Customer cannot access the application, API, or internal administration console.

Provider will maintain redundant infrastructure in two geographically distinct availability zones to ensure continuity. Load balancers, network edge components, and storage nodes shall all operate in active-active configuration.

---------------------------------------
2. INCIDENT CLASSIFICATION AND RESPONSE
---------------------------------------
Incidents are classified into Severity 1, Severity 2, and Severity 3.

- Severity 1: complete downtime, data unavailability, or major transaction failure. Response time: 1 hour.  
- Severity 2: degraded performance or partial feature outage. Response time: 4 hours.  
- Severity 3: minor issues with limited operational impact. Response: next business day.

Provider shall work continuously on Severity 1 issues until resolution.

---------------------------------------
3. PERFORMANCE METRICS
---------------------------------------
Provider shall adhere to the following performance metrics:  
- API response time < 450ms for 95th percentile requests  
- Database replication lag < 3 seconds  
- Backup completion reliability ≥ 99.5%  
- Release rollback time ≤ 10 minutes during deployment failures  

Metrics shall be logged using Provider's monitoring tools and made visible to Customer via dashboard access.

---------------------------------------
4. SECURITY AND DATA PROTECTION
---------------------------------------
Provider shall implement industry-standard controls including data encryption at rest (AES-256), TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit, firewall policies, intrusion detection, role-based access controls, and quarterly penetration tests.

In the event of a suspected breach, Provider shall notify Customer within six (6) hours.

---------------------------------------
5. SERVICE CREDITS
---------------------------------------
If uptime falls below 99.9%, Customer is eligible for monthly service credits:
- 99.0–99.89% → 10% credit  
- 95.0–98.99% → 25% credit  
- below 95.0% → 50% credit  

Credits shall be applied to the next invoice.

---------------------------------------
6. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
---------------------------------------
Provider’s total liability is capped at the fees paid by Customer in the preceding twelve (12) months. Provider is not liable for indirect, special, or consequential damages.

---------------------------------------
7. TERMINATION
---------------------------------------
Either party may terminate for material breach if not cured within thirty (30) days. Customer may terminate immediately if Provider fails to meet uptime guarantees for three consecutive months.

---------------------------------------
8. MISCELLANEOUS
---------------------------------------
8.1 Force Majeure  
Neither party is liable for delays caused by events beyond reasonable control.

8.2 Governing Law  
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
